## Intersecting Circles solution

A Circle class will have coordinates, a method to change its own color, and a method to compare it to other circles and detect intersecting perimeters.

### Task At Hand	

Create a function to loop through all circles at any time, and given a list of Circles, will update their colors depending on whether they do or do not intersect.
